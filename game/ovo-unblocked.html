How to Play OvO

Controls

Use the arrow keys to move your character. You can customize the controls in the options menu to suit your preference.

Gameplay

Navigate through each level by utilizing various parkour moves:

  • Jump to overcome gaps and obstacles
  • Slide under low passages
  • Wall jump to reach higher platforms
  • Dive to quickly descend or avoid obstacles

Tips & Tricks

  • Timing is crucial - practice your jumps carefully
  • Collect all coins to unlock new character skins
  • Start with easy mode if you're finding the game too challenging
  • Use wall jumps to gain height quickly
  • Don't give up! The game is designed to be challenging but rewarding
